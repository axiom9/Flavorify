Flavorify v1 Help

This is a a tool that will add some flavor and life to your texts, tweets, emails, etc by the use of common emojis. I mean come on it's 2023 don't be dry 😉 instead use Flavorify 😍


Instructions:

Enter in your sentence with proper punctuation (as the splitting occurs on punctuations in the middle of the sentence and once at the end)
ex: Such a beautiful sunny day today! God bless America --> Such a beautiful sunny day today 😎 God bless America 🇺🇸.

Note that currently supported punctuation is periods, commas, exclamation marks and question marks.
